How We Make an Impact

Volunteering

Volunteering is when someone chooses to give their time and labor, often for community service.

Volunteering to work with animals

Volunteering to work with animals can be a hobby. Opportunities include: looking after injured animals, working with community farms, monitoring local wildlife, working with stray and feral cats or dogs, and helping with the 200.

Green volunteering

Green volunteering: There are many different ways to get involved through volunteering including community work and initiatives such as: wildlife conservations, environmental education projects, and recycling.

Volunteering for a community project

Volunteers can make a difference in your local area by donating time which is a few hours each week. Also, many locals offer commitment-free options for people with limited time.

Volunteer in hospitals

Volunteering in hospitals allows you to contribute to the healthcare system by helping patients, assisting medical staff, and providing emotional support to those in need. Whether you're helping with administrative work, delivering meals to patients, or offering companionship, hospital volunteers play a vital role in supporting patients' well-being. It's a rewarding way to make a positive impact on the community.
